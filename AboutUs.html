History

1994

Sandiwara Indah Sdn Bhd is founded by the Liong family, at that time the company only does bandsaw wood cutting for square logs instead of the large wooden log with the wooden furniture license.

2000

After getting the sawmill license the company is then allowed to process wood logs for the different bigger products, which is called sawn timber.

2003

The second and third factory is opened for finishing product, which is mainly focusing in timber moulding. These factories provide a new variation in the production line, which not only wooden plank, other types of fine products can also be created in Sandiwara Indah. At the same time, the company also started to export products to other countries and regions, such as Japan, China, Korea, and Europe.

2010

With years of experience in wood processing, the company is then creating furniture for the locals, including plywood doors, outdoor tables, indoor tables, flowerpot stands, and others...

2020

Due to the covid 19 pandemic, to keep our employees healthy, Sandiwara Indah follows every SOP provided by the government, such as maintaining social distance and reducing the number of workers at a time. But our production line will be still running depends on the demand of the market until today.
